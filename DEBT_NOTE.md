## How Debt Note Works Inside Flynt

### 1. Intent Capture (User Input)

User enters:

* Debtor name
* Amount owed
* Deadline
* Priority level

This gives Flynt **constraints**, not just information.

---

### 2. System Analysis (Flynt Intelligence)

Flynt evaluates:

* Current account balance
* Incoming income
* Budget allocations
* Active subscriptions & leakages
* Upcoming essentials
* Risk of missing the deadline

This uses the **same data Flynt already controls**.

---

### 3. Decision Logic (Core Value)

Flynt recommends one of the following:

* **Pay now**
  If liquidity is healthy and no critical leakages remain

* **Delay strategically**
  If clearing leakages first creates a better outcome

* **Partial payment**
  If full payment would break essentials or savings

* **Escalate priority**
  If deadline risk outweighs short-term optimization

This aligns with Flynt‚Äôs core promise:

> *Make the best financial decision before money moves.*
